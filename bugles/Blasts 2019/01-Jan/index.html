18th January 2019 Trip to Leek.
 
Combined with a visit to the Holy Inadequate at Etruria, and a stroll up to Hanley duck to visit Mart’s Brewhouse in Piccadilly.
 
12 of us eventually made it, with Bolt and Nut (Bolt’s Dad) missing the bus and Craigee and Nails stopping over at the Premiier Inn.
 
The snow didn’t put us off as we then started at The Earl Grey, followed by The Fountain Inn and the recently opened Old Mill Smokehouse. The Pabna didn’t disappoint to round it all off.
